ABOUT

PUSB is a multi-discipline engineering consultancy to provide design engineering and project management services to the oil and gas, petrochemical and building industries.

In addition to servicing the Oil companies through direct contracts, PUSB has extensive experience in working with both international and local contractors either as Contractor or Alliance Partner in undertaking EPIC contract.

PUSB employs professional engineers, draftspersons and technical support personnel. The manpower level varies depending on the workload.

Our resources enable us to provide a complete range of engineering service for small to large sized projects; from conceptual design and feasibility studies, planning, budget preparation, detailed design and drafting through to management of construction and commissioning.

When necessary, PUSB is able to provide further resources and expertise through its alliance with specialist local and international firms.
